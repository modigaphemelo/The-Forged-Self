03. Reclaim Focus

Mastering your forge's heat in an age of engineered distraction

Your attention is the fire of your forge.
Every distraction is heat stolen.
Every interruption is a strike interrupted.
This series is about becoming the blacksmith of your own focus -
deciding what gets heated, what gets shaped, what gets struck.

The Core Challenge

We've moved from an Attention Economy (where focus is traded) to a Distraction Industry (where focus is mined). The modern world is engineered to dissipate your forge's heat through constant interruption. Your willpower is a gentle bellows against industrial-strength distraction engineering.

The Forge Analogy

Your focus is forge heat. Every notification is a draft that cools the metal. Social media is slag that contaminates the ore. This series teaches you to protect your heat, fuel your fire intentionally, and shape with full temperature.
